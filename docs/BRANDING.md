# Structura – Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Product Name:** Structura

**Tagline:** *Transform Documents into Interactive Learning.*

**Product Vision:**
Structura is an AI-powered learning platform that transforms static documents into structured, interactive courses. Users can upload PDFs, technical documentation, research papers, books, or study material and instantly receive a complete learning experience with lessons, quizzes, progress tracking, and an AI tutor.

---

# Brand Personality

Structura should feel:

* Intelligent
* Modern
* Minimal
* Professional
* Trustworthy
* Fast
* Structured

The product should resemble modern AI SaaS platforms such as Notion, Linear, Cursor, Perplexity, and Vercel rather than a traditional Learning Management System.

---

# Brand Mission

Make any document easy to understand through AI-powered structured learning.

---

# Brand Promise

Every document can become an interactive learning experience.

---

# Core Design Principles

* Simplicity over clutter
* Structure over complexity
* AI should feel helpful, not magical
* Every screen should have a clear purpose
* Fast interactions with smooth feedback
* Consistent spacing, typography, and colors

# AI Processing Experience

Instead of showing a generic loading spinner, display progress steps.

```
✓ Document Uploaded

✓ Extracting Text

✓ Understanding Content

✓ Designing Course

✓ Writing Lessons

✓ Creating Quiz

✓ Preparing AI Tutor

✓ Course Ready
```

This makes AI processing feel transparent and engaging.

# Brand Summary

Structura is designed to feel like a premium AI SaaS product that transforms static knowledge into structured learning. Every interaction should reinforce simplicity, intelligence, and clarity, making learning feel effortless while maintaining a modern, professional aesthetic.
